About Us

This is m4n team, a young and an innovative group created by Addis Ababa Science and Technology University Students aiming to create a joint power toward problem solving and income generating by producing ingenious software products.

This group was established with a mission to reach the multilateral problems of our country as well as our continent through collaborative effort toward one goal. The members of this group are well equipped with technical and psychological capabilities to challange any hard situation along the journey. We employed collaboration as the major equipment of our team to overcome barriers and to found our team on a sustainable foundation.

We hope you enjoy using our products and we rely on your comments and suggestions to improve them continually.
